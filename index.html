About Me

I am a recent Computer Science graduate from the University of Colorado Boulder. I am from a small town near São Paulo - Brazil, and have been living in the USA since I started studying at CU in Fall 2022. I majored in Computer Science with a B.A, and also did 2 minors: one in Computer Engineering and one in Business. If you're curious about the decision behind these minors, there are a couple good reasons. First, I did a Bachelor of Arts program, so I was required to have at least one additional area of study. I chose Computer Engineering because I think it gives me a more complete outlook into programs, since I now have a more structured understanding of how things work inside a computer. And I also chose to do the Business Minor because I believe it gives me a different view into things, one that's technical, but doesn't limit itself to that, giving me a more diversified perspective.
