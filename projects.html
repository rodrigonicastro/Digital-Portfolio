Projects & Academic Work

Connect 4

This is the final project for my Embedded Software Engineering class. Using a STM32 board and the peripherals in it, I developed a game of Connect 4 using the touch screen and buttons. The application is fully coded in C, with help of the HAL (Hardware Abstraction Layer) to configure and initialize all of the needed peripherals.

Fridge Fusion

In a group of 4 students, we developed a website that takes in the ingredients you have in your fridge and makes recipe suggestions based on that. You can also add friends and combine ingredients with them to get customized recipes based on both of your ingredients. There is a full description of our project and technology stack in this GitHub repository.

IMDB Simulator

Used several data structures to develop a simulation of IMDB's functionalities, with a database of around 1000 movies. All of the code was written in C++, including Hash Tables, Graphs, and Skip Lists, to allow searches for directors, actors, movie titles, etc.
